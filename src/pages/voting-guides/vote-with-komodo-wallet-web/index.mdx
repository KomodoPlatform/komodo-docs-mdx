# How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web

1. Launch [Komodo Wallet Desktop](h) and log in to your wallet. If required, [import your seed phrase](https://forum.komodoplatform.com/t/how-to-import-wallet-in-atomicdex-web/653).

2. **KIP** or **VOTE** tokens should be automatically activated on login. From within the Portfolio tab, click on the vote token. These tokens will be named **KIPxxxx** or **VOTExxxx**, with the xxxx portion being an identifying number to indicate which KIP or notary season vote the token represents.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb1} alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

3. Click the **Send** button to open the withdrawal form.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb2} alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

4. Review the [poll options](https://github.com/KomodoPlatform/kips) \(for KIPs\) or [candidates](https://github.com/KomodoPlatform/notaryNodes) \(for notary node elections\) to find the associated address for the candidate or option you would like to support, then input this address into the **Address of recipient** field at the top, and enter the amount of votes to send in the lower field.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb3} alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

5. Click **Send** to review your vote transaction before sending. If you are happy with the details, click **Confirm** to cast your vote!

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb4} alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

6. Once the transaction has been broadcast, you will see a summary of the transaction.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb5} alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

7. Click the **View on Explorer** button to see your vote on the block explorer.
