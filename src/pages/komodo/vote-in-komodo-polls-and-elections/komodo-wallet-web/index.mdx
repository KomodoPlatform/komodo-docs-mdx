# How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web

1.  Launch [Komodo Wallet Web](https://app.komodoplatform.com/) and log in to your wallet. If required, [import your seed phrase](https://forum.komodoplatform.com/t/how-to-import-wallet-in-atomicdex-web/653) using the **Connect Wallet** button.

2.  **KIP** or **VOTE** tokens should be automatically activated on login (If not, use the **Add Assets** button). From within the Wallet tab, click on the vote token. These tokens will be named **KIPxxxx** or **VOTExxxx**, with the xxxx portion being an identifying number to indicate which KIP or notary season vote the token represents.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb1} classNaming="w-full" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

3.  Click the **Send** button to open the withdrawal form.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb2} classNaming="w-full" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

4.  Review the [poll options](https://github.com/KomodoPlatform/kips) (for KIPs) or [candidates](https://github.com/KomodoPlatform/notaryNodes) (for notary node elections) to find the associated address for the candidate or option you would like to support, then input this address into the **Recipient address** field at the top, and enter the amount of votes to send in the lower field.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb3} classNaming="w-full" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

5.  Click **Send** to review your vote transaction before sending. If you are happy with the details, click **Confirm** to cast your vote!

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb4} classNaming="w-full" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

6.  Once the transaction has been broadcast, you will see a summary of the transaction.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb5} classNaming="w-full" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />

7.  Click the **View on Explorer** button to see your vote on the block explorer.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" src={voteKomodoWalletWeb6} classNaming="w-full" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Web" />
