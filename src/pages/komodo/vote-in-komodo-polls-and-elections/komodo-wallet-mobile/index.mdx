# How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile

1.  Launch [Komodo Wallet Mobile](https://atomicdex.io/en/mobile/) and log in to your wallet. If required, [import your seed phrase](https://forum.komodoplatform.com/t/how-to-restore-wallet-from-your-seed-using-atomicdex-mobile/412).

2.  **KIP** and **VOTE** tokens should be automatically activated on login. From within the Portfolio tab, click on the vote token. These tokens will be named **KIPxxxx** or **VOTExxxx**, with the **xxxx** portion being an identifying number to indicate which KIP or notary season vote the token represents.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" src={voteKomodoWalletMobile1} classNaming="w-300" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" />

3.  Click the **Send** button to open the withdrawal form.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" src={voteKomodoWalletMobile2} classNaming="w-300" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" />

4.  Review the [poll options](https://github.com/KomodoPlatform/kips) (for KIPs) or [candidates](https://github.com/KomodoPlatform/notaryNodes) (for notary node elections) to find the associated address for the candidate or option you would like to support, then input this address into the **Recipient's address** field at the bottom, and enter the amount of votes to send in the top **Amount** field.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" src={voteKomodoWalletMobile3} classNaming="w-300" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" />

5.  Click **Withdraw** to review your vote transaction before sending. If you are happy with the details, click **Confirm** to cast your vote!

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" src={voteKomodoWalletMobile4} classNaming="w-300" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" />

6.  Once the transaction has been broadcast, you will see a new transaction appear in your wallet history.

<OptimizedImage title="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" src={voteKomodoWalletMobile5} classNaming="w-300" alt="How to Vote on Komodo Wallet Mobile" />

7.  Click on it to view the transaction details. Click the **View** on **Explorer** button at the top right to see your vote on the block explorer.
